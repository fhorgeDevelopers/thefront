Why become an Fhorge Course Creator?


  • You can make a difference in the world by sharing your knowledge and expertise with a global audience who are keen to learn.
  • Earn an income by monetizing your skills and experience through our shared revenue offering.
  • Showcase your skills and build your personal brand as an expert in your field.
  • Expand your reach across emerging markets. Over 85% of Fhorge learners live outside developed economies
  • Use your knowledge and expertise to create and publish courses that will help you earn a passive income for your future.

How do I become a Course Creator on Fhorge?

It’s easier than you may think! If you have expertise in your field, excellent training material that you have developed and you know how to lead online learners through a fulfilling course, we want to hear from you. Complete the application form and let’s start the Course Creating journey together.

What makes a successful Course Creator?

  • Excellent English language writing skills.
  • Expert knowledge in a sought-after field.
  • The ability to accurately and quickly distill lots of information into a series of modules using text, images and videos.

Who does Course Creating suit best?

Anyone who meets our strict criteria for quality and expertise can be a Course Creator. Many members of our sCourse Creating education family are:

  • Retired, part-time or even full-time lecturers, teachers or researchers.
  • Senior professionals with a passion for training, mentoring or teaching.
  • Experts who want to find another channel or platform to extend their business.

How profitable is Course Creating?


Like many things in life, what you put in, is what you get out. Our courses are free to a global audience of learners and they don’t have to have any prior qualifications before embarking on a course that interests them. Theoretically, any of our more than 19 million users could be a learner of yours.

In our experience, a publisher can expect each course to earn somewhere in the range of €100 - €250 euros per month during the first 12 months.

Some things to consider when considering creating a course:

  • The more in demand your topic, the more learners you will attract to your course.
  • The higher learners rank your course, the more income you are likely to generate. Learners want courses that are high quality, relevant, interesting, useful, accurate and up-to-date.
  • The more teaching collateral you include – such as well-produced videos, webinars, imagery, downloadable PDFs – the more engagement you will have.
  • Our most profitable Course Creators use their other online accounts to promote their courses. This is something you could do too!

What support do you we offer Course Creators?


We are committed to partnering with you every step of the way. We run regular webinars for Course Creators , where you can connect with an Fhorge Trainer and ask all your questions. Once you have submitted a test course, you will be assigned a publishing account manager who will guide you through the process of publishing your first course.

Once your course is published, then the marketing magic will kick in. We will promote your course with paid advertising on social media and search. We have an extensive global community of over 19 million learners, and we will use email marketing to reach them.

Your personalised digital dashboard will help you track how your course enrolments are progressing and give you insights on your learners’ location and their devices

What does the Qualified Fhorge Publishing training programme offer?

Fhorge developed a training programme to create entrepreneurial opportunity for those who would like to build and publish online courses. Your journey to becoming a fully Qualified Fhorge Publisher happens in three stages.

  • Level 1 is achieved once your application is submitted and you have successfully completed the required training courses.
  • Level 2 is when your work meets strict quality standards and you create and publish your first course on the Fhorge website.
  • Level 3 is when you publish four or more high-quality courses on the Fhorge website.

Steps to start earning as a Course Creator

1. Sign up

Sign up for an Fhorge account

2. Complete the Application Form

Tell us about yourself by completing the Course Creators application form . Once we have your details we will take you through each step of the publishing journey.

3. Gain Access to the Publishing Tool

Once you have submitted the publishing application form, you will receive an email with a link to access the Publishing Tool.

4. Complete the Training Courses

Before you start creating your course, you first need to complete two free training courses. The first training module is E-Learning Theory and Practice and outlines how to create an online course from a pedagogic point of view. You will learn how to structure your course and how to keep the learner engaged through content. The second training module is the Fhorge Publisher Tool Training Course , and it explains how to use the publishing tool. Each course will take you about 90 minutes to complete.

5. Build Your Sample Course

When you have completed the steps above, you will receive some content to create a sample course. This will give you a flavour of what publishing is all about. Download this simple step-by-step guide on how to use the publishing tool.

6. Submit Your Sample Course

You will not receive feedback for this sample course build, but you will be notified by email if your sample course passed our quality standard checklist to progress your application.

7. Create Your Own Course

If your sample course is approved, you may now begin to create your own course using the skills you have learnt from the Course Creator training process. You will be assigned a Publishing Account Manager (PAM) who will review your course and provide you with feedback to ensure that your course is ready for publishing. Once your course meets the Fhorge standard, it will be published.

8. Course Advertising and Marketing

Once your course is published, we will advertise your course to a global audience and to our learner database. There are many activities and tactics available to us to make sure your course gets the attention of as many learners as possible.

9. Monitor Course Performance

Once your course is published, you will be able to track its performance. We will give you access to a link through which you can review your course’s daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly or annual performance(s). Our standard format of reporting covers the following: number of visitors, active learners, enrols, completes and certificate purchases. We will also give you a record of your advertising and certification revenue which you are entitled to.

10. Receive Payment

With your initial publishing and marketing work done, you can sit back and earn 24/7 from interested learners who are accessing your course(s) online. It is important to note that it does take up to 24 months before you can earn a regular income. Of course, you can augment your earnings even more by constantly marketing your course to your personal network and by publishing new courses.
